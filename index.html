What is DropZone?

Dropzone is a random locaction generator that will help you and your squad decide where to drop in Warzone.

Features

There are a few features here that will help you customise your pool of drop locactions. Just click on the 'Custom Drops & Features' button above to customise your drops.

There is also no custom location limit, meaning you can have as many as you want. You can even remove all default locations and replace them with customs.

No Repeat Option

This feature will allow you to create random drops without any duplictes and will stop when you run out of drop locactions. You then have to press 'Reset' to start again. The toggle for this is in the features section.
